iF Design Award 2019

Professional Concept Category

Stool.D designed by Sangjin Park, won iF Design Award 2019 - a world-renowned design prize. The winning product, stool.D is a cycling enabled stool, and won in the discipline Professional Concept category. Each year, the world’s oldest independent design organization, Hannover-based iF International Forum Design GmbH, organizes the iF DESIGN AWARD. stool.D won over the 67-member jury, made up of independent experts from all over the world. The competition was intense: 6,375 entries were submitted from 52 countries in hopes of receiving the seal of quality

Spark Design Award: Spring Concept

Maze Cube (Winner - Gold) 2017

Maze Cube’ is a transformable 3D cubic maze for children around 10 years old. It allows to put a ball into the entrance and move the ball by rotating the ‘Maze Cube’, then the ball comes out of the exit. ‘Maze Cube’ can raise children’s abilities about gravity, spatial perception and concentration.
For more detail, please visit http://www.sparkawards.com/
