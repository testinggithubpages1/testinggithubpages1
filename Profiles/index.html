About Me

Who Am I?

I am KwanMyung Kim, an associate professor at Graduate School of Creative Design Engineering UNIST and the director of Integration and Innovation Design Lab (IID). I have recieved my Bachelor's, Master's and PhD Degrees in Industrial Design from KAIST, Daejeon South Korea.

With 14 years of industry experience, I intend to bring practical knowledge on design and design engineering in academic research. I am interested in combining and generating design and engineering knowledge that lead innovative and practical outcomes through design research. Currently, my research interests include design-integrated innovation, innovative product development, and integrated design process
